View all or a specific announcement

I want to keep track of all the announcements I have created. Will I be able to do that?

Absolutely! AnnounceIT enables you to view all your announcements. You can also view a specific announcement and its details.

View announcement of a specific state

As an advertiser, I want to know which announcements were accepted or declined. Will I be able to do that?

AnnounceIT allows you to filter your announcements by their state. For instance, you can view all accepted announcements or all pending announcements.
